Twitch Bot Developer

I am seeking a highly skilled developer to join my team in building and maintaining a multi-purpose Twitch bot. The successful candidate will have experience in developing and implementing bots on Twitch and will have a strong understanding of the Twitch API.

  • Collaborate with team members to design and develop new features for the bot
  • Maintain and update existing features of the bot
  • Troubleshoot and fix bugs as they arise
  • Ensure the bot is running smoothly and efficiently
  • Stay up-to-date with Twitch API changes and updates
  • Work with team members to optimize the bot for best performance

Qualifications

  • Familiarity with Git and version control
  • Strong experience with JavaScript and Node.js
  • Experience developing and implementing bots on Twitch
  • Strong understanding of the Twitch API
  • Junior Developer
  • Remote
  • 5/2023

What you can take to the interview:

  • Custom node.js or JavaScript Project
  • Curriculum vitae
